  • Create your open house strategy early - and make it detailed. Do a walk through of the home, perhaps during the listing photography session, take detailed notes and ask these questions: 

    • What are key property features to tell the story of the property? 

    • Where am I going to place the sign-in sheet or the Compass Open House app? What about printed materials? 

    • In what order will I show the rooms when guests arrive? 

    • Where are the most optimal places to hang signs guiding visitors to the open house? 

    • Will parking be an issue? 

    • Thinking through these details in advance will ensure you’re fully prepared when it comes to open house day.
  • Call or text seller to confirm schedule of events. Then, email to your seller outlining the steps for open house prep. Email template here.
  • List the open house on the MLS and verify open house time(s) are correct on all syndicated websites.
  • Email open house announcement to your contact list (brokers and potential buyers). Email template here.
  • Let your network know about the open house by sharing this asset on your social channels.
  • Order an "Open House Rider" or "Swing A-frame" sign through your regional signage request form on Navigation Center, or use Sign Center in regions where it is currently active.
  • Design and order showsheets from Marketing Center. Confirm that the show sheets from Marketing Center. Confirm that the showsheets and/or brochures have been printed accurately (correct price, bed/bath count, agent contact information, etc.)
  • Curate a playlist relevant to the upcoming open house that matches the vibe of the neighborhood.
